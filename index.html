MedMatch - Drug Interaction & Pharmacy Locator

About us:


MedMatch is a comprehensive healthcare platform designed to bridge the gap between patients, medicines, and pharmacies. The system provides a robust solution for searching medicines, checking drug-to-drug interactions, locating nearby pharmacies via interactive maps, and managing digital prescriptions. It ensures patient safety by identifying potentially harmful interactions and providing reliable information about medication availability.

MedMatch is a smart healthcare platform that helps patients search for medicines, locate nearby pharmacies, and manage digital prescriptions. The system provides accurate medicine information and makes it easier for users to access healthcare services through a simple and user-friendly interface.


Our Features

💊 Medicine Search

Search medicines instantly and get detailed information including dosage, manufacturer, strength, and usage.

⚠ Drug Interaction Checker

Check whether two or more medicines can be safely taken together and receive interaction warnings.

📍 Pharmacy Locator

Locate nearby pharmacies using an interactive map and check medicine availability.

📄 Digital Prescription

Store prescriptions digitally and access them anytime without carrying paper copies.
